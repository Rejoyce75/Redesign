Lionel Messi Profile


By Chris Stanton


Argentina's gifted forward Lionel Messi needs little introduction to followers of European football but Germany 2006 could mark his coming of age on the international stage. Born in Rosario, Sante Fe, the Barcelona schemer began his career at Newell's Old Boys but, in a bizarre twist of fate, came to the attention of the Catalunyans after his parents moved to Spain in a bid to cure their 11-year-old's hormone deficiency.
